Meet Tee Tee from Growing Up Hip Hop, the stars age and life explored

April 2024 · 3 minute read
Spread the love

Tahira Francis, affectionately known as Tee Tee, has become a familiar face in the world of reality TV, thanks to her consistent presence on Growing Up Hip Hop. Fans have grown fond of her, and there’s a curiosity to learn more about her age, profession, family life, and more. Let’s delve into the details and get to know Tee Tee better.

Tee Tee’s Age, Career, and Nationality

Tee Tee hails from the United States, with Jamaican heritage adding a rich cultural dimension to her background. She came into this world on May 28, 1990, which means she’s 31 years old this year.

But Tee Tee’s journey didn’t start in the television industry. She’s a well-educated individual who holds a degree from Sarah Lawrence College. Her academic prowess extends to being a talented linguist, having graduated with majors in Economics and Spanish.

After completing her college education, Tee Tee initially ventured into the field as a paralegal. However, she soon realized her aspirations reached further. Her determination led her to become an investment analyst, where she excelled in her role. But Tee Tee didn’t stop there.

Her ambition knew no bounds, and she eventually transitioned into the role of a wealth management consultant. She carved out a successful career in business development, working for Davis Polk and Wardwell.

Tee Tee’s Wedding Celebration

Fans of Tee Tee were thrilled and pleasantly surprised when she and her partner, Shawn Rogers, tied the knot. Shawn proposed to his beloved on the show, marking a beautiful chapter in their relationship.

The couple’s journey together culminated in a memorable wedding on November 20, 2021, at the Palm Beach Marriot Singer Island Beach Resort in Florida. Tee Tee, resplendent in a gown by Ines De Santo from Lovella Bridal in LA, looked stunning on her special day.

Their love story continued to blossom after they welcomed their first child, Laith, into the world. For those who want to relive the moments leading up to the wedding, including the heartwarming proposal, Season 5 of Growing Up Hip Hop holds all the cherished memories.

Tee Tee’s Instagram Presence

Tee Tee is an active presence on social media, with a dedicated following of 112K followers. You can catch up with her adventures and updates on her Instagram account, where she goes by the handle @tee_fran.

Her Instagram feed offers a delightful glimpse into her life, featuring adorable snapshots of her son, her loving husband, and their travels and family outings.

Conclusion

Tee Tee, or Tahira Francis, is more than just a reality TV star. Her journey from her educational pursuits to her career as a wealth management consultant showcases her determination and ambition. Her recent wedding and the addition of a new member to her family have made her life even more fulfilling.
