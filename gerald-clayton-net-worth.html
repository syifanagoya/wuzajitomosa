Educational Foundation and Early Influences

Gerald’s musical journey began under the influence of his father, John Clayton, a renowned jazz bassist.

His formal education in music started at the Los Angeles County High School for the Arts, followed by intensive studies at USC’s Thornton School of Music under the mentorship of Billy Childs and at the Manhattan School of Music with Kenny Barron.
